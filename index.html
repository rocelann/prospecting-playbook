Who Made This Thing

My name is John Schuette. I'm not a career coach guru thing. I just can't fathom how people go so long without a sales role. Even more than a week baffles me.

I'm a mindset coach for experienced sales reps, business owners, and sales managers.

When I got started in sales it was a beautiful Missouri afternoon. I was driving around with my girlfriend at the time, and I had us drive to a Hyundai dealership. I picked Hyundai because I was scared of the big tough truck driving Ford guys and hoped I didn't have to face them at Hyundai.

I walked in at 19 years old, which is like 16 years younger than they typically hire for. They passed me through 4-5 different reps, managers, and finance guys, each interviewing me and trying to get me to buckle, and I didn't. I was there for 3 hours, my girlfriend STILL waiting in the car, and walked out with a work shirt and a job starting tomorrow.

Next I did insurance MLM, which ain't much, then got a job selling merchant services. Once I quit both of those I started selling pools, which I got because of targeted outreach on Indeed and Monster and ZipRecruiter. I showed up, got trained in a hotel lobby, and was selling shortly after, doing traveling sales. A new city each week.

While traveling, I applied to about 100 roles on Indeed daily. I knew this was a 2 to 3 month long role, so I was on it. I had interviews between sales calls, sitting in my car in my Hawaiian shirt, and I was stacking job offers. Many I was turning down because they wouldn't pay to relocate me, which was what I was looking for.

Then I got into online high ticket. Got a role because I was in the Traffic and Funnels high ticket closer certification thing, which was a fantastic program, and I never got certified, but I got a role selling ecom. Then the company got shut down. I started a sales agency for 2 weeks, then quit when I aggressively pushed my way into brokering apartments, and they paid me 4k upfront so I could move to Denver.

I slept on a couch for a month while doing that until I was poached by a friend to sell in his company. When that company shut down, I had a role that week through connections. Then another company poached me. Then that sales team got shut down, so I got a referral through a friend and was selling the following week.

Point is, you're the product. I was my own product, and I knew how to sell me because I had unreasonable confidence. And I did high volume to ensure I had an abundance of opportunities. Then, after a while, opportunities flowed to me easily.

So regardless of where you're at, don't wait for opportunities to come to you. Go get em.

This process will work for you IF you apply it. It's literally a high volume, outbound sales process across many platforms to land a $100K to $500K/year sales role. You do the qualification. You do the outreach.

I just show you how, so you don't sit around on your ass anymore.
